INFOSYS Institute of Information
Technology Sargodha

INFOSYS Institute of Information Technology is the first institute in Sargodha which offers the latest ICT (Information & Communication Technology) trainings & courses. We are committed to our student’s satisfaction and have created a very friendly and learning environment with latest hardware and software. Our labs are equipted with latest operating systems and the software. INFOSYS Institute of Information Technology is official partner of Cisco US in Sargodha and working as Cisco Networking Academy. INFOSYS IIT is also official partner of E-Pakistan and working as Province Technology Center (PTC) for Punjab Province. Whether you are new to the ICT industry or are to improve your skills, INFOSYS IIT is the right place for you.
